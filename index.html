About Shahran Foundation

Serving humanity with dignity, equality, and compassion.

Shahran Foundation is a Public Charitable Trust registered in Hazur Nagar, Muzaffarnagar, Uttar Pradesh. Established on 20th May 2026, the foundation is dedicated to healthcare, education, social welfare, environmental protection, and spiritual upliftment.


The Trust operates without discrimination based on caste, creed, race, sex, religion, or place of birth. Every initiative and service is accessible equally to all members of society.
